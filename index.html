Elena Alegret


I am a Master’s student at ETH Zurich specializing in 3D computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on 3D scene understanding and reconstruction. Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Artificial Intelligence at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), including a one-year exchange at Technical University of Munich (TUM). During my B.S.c, I interned at TUM in 2025, EPFL in 2024, and the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC) in 2023-24.

For any inquiries, feel free to reach out to me via mail!

CV Mail Twitter Scholar Github LinkedIn

Awards: In 2025, I graduated with Highest Honors for my Bachelor’s thesis at TU Munich (TUM), supervised by Prof. Federico Tombari; the results of this work have been accepted at 3DV 2026. In 2021, I was selected to present my high-school research project at Invesjove. In 2021, I received the Academic Excellence Award (Catalonia) for achieving a score above 9/10 in the Spanish national university entrance exams (PAU), with an overall score of 13.006/14.
