Elena Alegret is a Master’s student at ETH Zurich specializing in 3D computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on 3D scene understanding and reconstruction. She completed her BSc in Artificial Intelligence at UPC, including a year-long exchange at TU Munich (TUM). During a research internship at TUM, she worked on GALA, a 3D Gaussian Splatting–based approach for open-vocabulary 3D scene understanding (accepted at 3DV 2026). She has also held research internships at EPFL and the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC).
